1. Service Overview

SwimPilot is an app for tracking and analyzing swimming workouts.

Features

  • Swimming workout tracking and recording
  • Lap counting via Apple Watch sensors
  • Workout time, distance, and calorie statistics
  • Apple HealthKit integration

Premium Features

  • Cloud backup (sync your records across devices)
  • Ad removal

2. Subscription & Payment

Subscription Plans

  • Monthly Subscription: Auto-renews monthly
  • Annual Subscription: Auto-renews annually

Auto-Renewal

Subscriptions automatically renew 24 hours before the current period ends and are charged to your Apple account.

Cancellation

You can cancel at least 24 hours before the subscription ends. After cancellation, premium features remain available until the current period expires.

Manage Subscription: iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → SwimPilot

3. Refund Policy

All payments and refunds are processed through the Apple App Store.

Requesting a Refund

To request a refund, visit Apple Support.

Refund Limitations

  • Used subscription periods are non-refundable
  • Premium services remain active until the current period ends, and that period is non-refundable

4. Privacy

SwimPilot collects the following information:

  • Swimming workout records (distance, time, laps, etc.)
  • Apple Watch sensor data (accelerometer, heart rate, etc.)
  • Apple HealthKit data (if you grant permission)

Data Storage

  • Free Users: Stored on device only
  • Premium Users: Device + cloud backup

Data Deletion

Deleting the app removes data from your device.

For cloud data deletion details, please refer to the Privacy Policy.

Note: This app is a fitness tracking tool and not a medical device. Sensor data accuracy depends on Apple Watch hardware performance and usage environment.
